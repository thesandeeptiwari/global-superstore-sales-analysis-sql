# Global Superstore Sales Analysis using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Global Superstore dataset using SQL to uncover valuable business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, discount impact, and geographical trends.

The analysis was performed using SQLiteStudio and includes 20 business-focused SQL queries designed to answer real-world business questions.

![Project Preview](Query%20Results%20Screenshots/q01_total_records.png)
---

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-selling and most profitable products
* Discover top customers and customer trends
* Evaluate category and sub-category performance
* Analyze sales across countries, cities, markets, and regions
* Study the impact of discounts on profitability
* Generate executive-level business insights

## Key Business Insights

* The dataset contains over 51,000 sales transactions.
* A small group of customers contributes significantly to overall revenue.
* Certain products generate high sales but low profitability.
* Discount-heavy products often reduce profit margins.
* Some products consistently generate losses.
* Sales performance varies significantly across markets and regions.
* Product and category analysis helps identify growth opportunities.
