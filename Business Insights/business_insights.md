# Business Insights

## Key Findings

* Total records analyzed: 51,290
* The dataset contains sales transactions from multiple countries and markets.
* Technology and Office Supplies generated significant sales revenue.
* Several products generated negative profits despite strong sales.
* A small group of customers contributed a large share of total revenue.
* Some regions consistently outperformed others in sales and profitability.
* High discounts were often associated with lower profit margins.

## Recommendations

* Reduce excessive discounting on low-margin products.
* Focus marketing efforts on profitable product categories.
* Strengthen relationships with top-performing customers.
* Expand sales strategies in high-performing markets.
* Monitor loss-making products and optimize pricing strategies.
* Use profitability analysis alongside sales analysis for better decision-making.

## Conclusion

The Global Superstore dataset reveals valuable insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends. SQL analysis helps transform raw sales data into actionable business intelligence for decision-making.
